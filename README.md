# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn patterns and retention trends to identify the key factors influencing customer attrition. The objective is to help businesses understand customer behavior, reduce churn, and improve long-term customer retention.

---

## Objectives
- Measure overall churn and retention performance.
- Identify customer segments with high churn risk.
- Analyze the impact of contract type, tenure, payment method, and services on churn.
- Provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

---

## Dashboard Components

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Retained Customers
- Churn Rate (%)
- Average Monthly Charges

### Visualizations
1. Churn Distribution
2. Churn by Contract Type
3. Churn by Payment Method
4. Churn by Internet Service
5. Churn by Gender
6. Churn by Tenure Group
7. Senior Citizen vs Churn Analysis
8. Monthly Charges vs Churn Analysis

### Filters
- Gender
- Contract Type
- Internet Service
- Senior Citizen Status

---

## Key Insights

### Contract Type Impact
Customers on month-to-month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn rates compared to customers on one-year and two-year contracts.

### Tenure Analysis
Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn, while long-term customers demonstrate stronger retention.

### Payment Method Analysis
Certain payment methods show higher churn rates, indicating potential friction in billing or customer commitment.

### Service-Based Churn
Internet service type influences customer retention, with some services experiencing greater customer loss than others.

### Customer Demographics
Demographic factors such as senior citizen status contribute to varying churn behavior across customer groups.

### Revenue Impact
Customers with higher monthly charges tend to have increased churn risk, highlighting potential pricing concerns.

---

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Promote Long-Term Contracts
Offer discounts, loyalty benefits, and exclusive features to encourage customers to move from month-to-month plans to annual contracts.

### 2. Strengthen Early Customer Engagement
Implement onboarding programs and proactive customer support during the first year of service to reduce early churn.

### 3. Optimize Pricing Strategies
Introduce personalized offers, bundled plans, and flexible pricing models for customers with higher monthly charges.

### 4. Improve Payment Experience
Encourage automatic payments and electronic billing to increase convenience and reduce churn associated with payment-related issues.

### 5. Enhance Service Quality
Investigate high-churn service categories and improve customer experience through better support and service reliability.

### 6. Develop Churn Prediction Models
Use customer attributes such as tenure, contract type, monthly charges, and service usage to identify at-risk customers before they leave.

### 7. Launch Targeted Retention Campaigns
Create personalized retention strategies for high-risk customer segments based on behavioral and demographic characteristics.
