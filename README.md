Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer retention and churn behavior to identify factors that influence customer attrition. The objective is to help businesses understand why customers leave and provide data-driven strategies to improve retention.

Business Questions Answered
What percentage of customers have churned?
Which contract types experience the highest churn?
Does internet service type influence churn behavior?
Which payment methods are associated with higher churn?
How does customer tenure affect retention?
Are senior citizens more likely to churn?
Is there any relationship between monthly charges and churn?
Key Insights
Contract type strongly influences retention.
Customers with shorter tenure are generally more likely to leave.
Higher monthly charges can contribute to churn risk.
Payment methods show varying retention patterns.
Customer demographics provide additional churn indicators.

Outcome

The dashboard helps organizations identify high-risk customer segments and supports strategic retention initiatives to reduce churn and improve customer lifetime value.
